Caprese Salad

Description

Caprese Salad is a classic Italian dish that highlights the simple yet harmonious flavors of fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil. Drizzled with balsamic glaze, itâ€™s a refreshing and elegant appetizer or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1 bunch fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Balsamic glaze for drizzling

Steps

  1. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices alternately on a serving platter.
  2. Tuck whole basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. Drizzle the olive oil over the salad.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Drizzle with balsamic glaze just before serving.
