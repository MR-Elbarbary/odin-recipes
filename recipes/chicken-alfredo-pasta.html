Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a rich and creamy dish featuring tender chicken breast pieces and fettuccine pasta coated in a luscious Alfredo sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken strips, season with salt and pepper, and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  5. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  6. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  7. Add the butter and stir until melted.
  8. Return the chicken to the skillet and stir to coat in the sauce.
  9. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss to combine.
  10. Sprinkle with chopped parsley before serving.
